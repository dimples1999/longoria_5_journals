Orange

Orange is a warm color.

The color orange shows cheerfullness, freedom, and enthusiasm. People who like orange can be friendly, confident, have good instinct and gut reaction. Orange is a close relative of red. Fun and flamboyant orange radiates warmth and energy and gives emotional strength in tough times. Softer tones are best for babies.
