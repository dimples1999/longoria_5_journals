Div Alignments

There are several div alignments.

    They are:
  • Center-moves text to the middle of the div.
  • Right-moves text to the right of the div.
  • Left-moves text to the left of the div.
  • Justify-spreads text evenly across the entire row.

    Div Positioning

  • This is a static div.

    A static div stays in place relative to the source code.
