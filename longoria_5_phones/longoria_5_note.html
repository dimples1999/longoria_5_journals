Galaxy Note

DESCRIPTION: A touchscreen Android smartphone that has the same capability that a tablet has. It was announced in October 2011 that this new phone is going to have a 5.3-inch screen and that it would also come with its very own stylist tool.
It was later released; starting with Germany in late October 2011. It was released to many other countries after that. The screen also has Gorilla Glass just like the Samsung Galaxy S. It has an 8-megapixel back camera and a 2-megapixel front camera.
