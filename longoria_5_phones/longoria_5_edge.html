Galaxy S6 edge 128GB

DESCRIPTION: A touchscreen Android smartphone also infused with a tablet marketed by Samsung Electronics. It has a 5.7-inch display around the phone's edges. The colors for this phone are available in "White Pearl", "Black Sapphire", "Gold Platnium", and "Silver Titanium".
The phone also has wireless charging capabilities. The back camera is 16 megapixels and has autofocus with LED flash.
