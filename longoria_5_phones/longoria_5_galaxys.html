Galaxy S

DESCRIPTION: A touchscreen Android smartphone marketed by Samsung Electronics. The first phone of the third Android smartphone series to be produced by Samsung. The Galaxy S was announced in March 2010 to be released for sale in June 2010.
Comes with either 8GB, 16GB, or 512GB, but a MicroSD card slot can hold up to 32GB of more storage. The touch screen is covered with Gorilla Glass used to try to help prevent cracks and scratches. It has a 5 megapixel camera and has digital zoom, not optical.
