Galaxy S6 64GB

DESCRIPTION: A touchscreen Android smartphone also infused with a tablet marketed by Samsung Electronics. This phone was released April 10, 2015 in 20 different countries.
The colors for this phone are available in "White Pearl", "Black Sapphire", "Gold Platnium", and "Silver Titanium".
The phone also has wireless charging capabilities. The back camera is 16 megapixels and has autofocus with LED flash.
