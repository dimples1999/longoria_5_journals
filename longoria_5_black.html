Black

Black is a warm color.

The color black shows authority, seriousness, and power. Cons of this color are that it portrays death and evilness. People who like black can be protective, classy, formal, and dramatic. Some traits of this color are conservative and professional. A big pro is that it implies submission. It also makes shapes appear thinner and gives things a timeless look. Black symbolizes emptiness, yet people who like this color have strong emotions. People who dislike this color feel its too depressing because you have such a positive and happy outlook on life. Also if you don't like this color there could be a possibility that you are afraid of the dark or use to be when you were a child.
