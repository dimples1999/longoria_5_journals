White

White is a cool color.

The color white shows purity and innocence. Cons of this color are coldness and distance in a person. People who like white can be calm, balanced, and have neutral feelings. Some traits of this color are goodness, fresh, and clean. It also can mean that a person surrenders and it could mean they are truthful.
