Green

Green is a cool color.

The color green shows growth, wealth, balance, and clarity. Cons of this color are envy, jealousy, and a guilt of some sort. People who like green can be peaceful, relaxed, refreshed, positive, and generous. Some traits of this color are well-being, awareness, and good judgement. A big pro is that it's the easiest color on the eye and can improve vision. Green is an ideal backdrop in interior design cause it symbolizes nature. If you dislike the color green you don't like doing things the way that others do, you're your own person and you exclude yourself from social groups because you like things better that way.
